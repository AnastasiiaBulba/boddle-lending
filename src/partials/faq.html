FAQ

Is Boddle free to use?

Yes, it’s free for students and teachers. Optional paid features are available.

What ages is it best for?

Designed for kids in Kindergarten through 6th grade.

Is it safe for kids?

Absolutely — Boddle is COPPA-compliant and ad-free.

Does it align with curriculum standards?

Yes, all content is standards-aligned and built by educators.

Can parents track progress?

Yes, parents get access to detailed learning reports and insights.
